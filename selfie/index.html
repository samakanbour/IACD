Between January 15th and March 3rd of 2014, I collected personal data that could give insight into my lifestyle in Pittsburgh, PA. I used Nike+ FuelBand to record the number of steps I take every day and OpenPaths to record the places I visit. As for my spending information, I logged into my online bank account and exported my transactions.
Are the findings exciting? Not really, but they still highlight the benefits of my shopping addiction!
